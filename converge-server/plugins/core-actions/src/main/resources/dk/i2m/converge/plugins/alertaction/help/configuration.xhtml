Configuration of Alert Action

Properties

Property Description Example
Recipient (User) Users with this role in the news item will receive the alert Subeditor
Recipient (Role) Users with this role in general will receive the alert Editor
Message Message contained in the alert. Messages support HTML and template tags, see below $initiator.fullName$ sent you the story $newsitem.title$

Template Tags

Template tags must be surrounded by dollar signs ($), i.e. $templateTag$. Some template tags are objects available in CONVERGE. To access the property of the object append the tag with a dot (.) followed by the name of the property (case sensitive), see below.

Tag Description
initiator UserAccount (Object) of the user who initiated the workflow
initiator.username Username of the person who activated the workflow
initiator.fullName Full name of the person who activated the workflow
initiator.givenName Given name of the person who activated the workflow
initiator.surname Surname of the person who activated the workflow
initiator.jobTitle Job title of the person who activated the workflow
initiator.organisation Organisation of the person who activated the workflow
initiator.email E-mail address of the person who activated the workflow
initiator.phone Phone number of the person who activated the workflow
initiator.status Last status update of the person who activated the workflow
initiator.timeZone Time zone of the person who activated the workflow
initiator.preferredLanguage Last status update of the person who activated the workflow
newsitem NewsItem that is being processed in the workflow
newsitem.title Title/headline of the NewsItem being processed
newsitem.byLine By-line of the NewsItem being processed
newsitem.story Story of the NewsItem being processed
newsitem.brief Brief of the NewsItem being processed
newsitem.language Language (Object) of the NewsItem being processed
newsitem.outlet Outlet (Object) of the NewsItem being processed
newsitem.currentState WorkflowState (Object) of the NewsItem being processed

See JavaDoc for full listing of available properties for objects.
